General Guidelines

  • Every student participant must carry his/her college ID card. Entry without a valid ID is not allowed.
  • Entry is only allowed through Gate 1 of the campus. All other gates will be closed for entry.
  • Accommodation will only be provided to students having a valid Anwesha'18 ID.
  • Participants carrying laptops and other valuable items must declare their belongings at the Registration Desk of Anwesha’18 through the form provided. Disclosure of any prior undeclared belongings may result in impounding of those items.
  • Accommodation will strictly be on first come first serve basis.
  • You can also avail online accommodation here .
  • Prior accommodation (01/02/2018) can be availed only through online accommodation facility.
  • Priority will be given to students having a longer duration of stay.
  • Anwesha'18 will provide you with mattresses,blankets and pillows. You are encouraged to carry basic requirements for your stay.
  • Accommodation will be given only after it has been confirmed by the Hospitality committee.
  • Students not having confirmed accommodation inside the campus are not allowed to use the accommodation facilities.
  • The declared accommodation charges do not include meal.Though there will be various food joints available in campus during fest.Food will also be available in hostel mess on pay per meal basis.
  • In case of damage to the property of IIT Patna , hefty fine will be charged.
  • Alcohol, drugs and sharp objects are strictly prohibited inside the campus. Any other item if deemed unsafe will be confiscated.
  • Vehicles will not be allowed beyond the parking lot near Gate 1.
  • Anwesha'18 and IIT Patna will not be responsible for any mishaps that occur through the duration of stay for Anwesha'18.
  • In case of any discrepancy, the decision made by the Hospitality committee will be final and binding.

    Accomodation and Registration charges

    The fest and accommodation charges per person are as follows:
  • The registration fee is Rs 100. Registration is compulsory for entry to the fest and participating in the events.
  • The ticket for the Band night costs Rs 150. You can register yourself and buy the Band night tickets for Rs. 200
  • The price for accommodation for a day is Rs. 150. For three days, the price is Rs. 400
  • If you wish to stay for more than 3 days, you will be charged Rs.500

    Getting Here

  • The IIT Patna campus is located in Bihta, about 35 kms( about 1.5 hours) from Patna junction and 31km from Patna airport , 23 kms ( about 45 mins) from Danapur Railway Station and 5 kms ( about 15 mins) from Bihta railway station. The Campus is well connected by road to each of these major check points. You can readily hire a taxi to reach our campus.
